    How We Work

    From Vision to Reality

    Our 13-step process ensures no detail is overlooked. We guide you through every stage of planning, from the first conversation to the final farewell.

    At Aether Events, we believe that great events are built on great processes. Our carefully crafted 13-step methodology has been refined through hundreds of successful celebrations across Tamil Nadu.

    Whether you're planning an intimate birthday party or a grand 500-guest wedding, our process scales to match your needs. Each step is designed to reduce stress, increase clarity, and ensure a spectacular result.

    From the moment you reach out to the moment your celebration ends, you'll have a dedicated team working tirelessly behind the scenes. Here's exactly how we bring your vision to life.

    Start Your Journey
    The Process

    Our 13-Step Planning Methodology

    Every step designed to bring clarity, creativity, and precision to your event.

    Free Initial Consultation

    We meet (in person or virtually) to understand your vision, requirements, budget, and expectations. This no-obligation conversation helps us determine if we're the right fit for each other.

    Proposal & Custom Quote

    Based on our discussion, we create a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work, design concepts, vendor recommendations, and a transparent pricing breakdown tailored to your budget.

    Booking & Agreement

    Once you approve the proposal, we finalize the contract, collect the initial deposit, and officially reserve your date. Your dedicated event manager is assigned at this stage.

    Venue Selection & Booking

    We help you find and book the perfect venue — whether it's a grand banquet hall, a scenic outdoor location, or an intimate community space. We handle negotiations and site visits.

    Design & Theme Development

    Our creative team develops a comprehensive design concept including color palettes, décor styles, floral arrangements, lighting design, and thematic elements that reflect your personality.

    Vendor Selection & Coordination

    We tap into our network of 50+ trusted vendors to recommend and book photographers, caterers, entertainers, florists, decorators, and more. We handle all contracts and coordination.

    Budget Management

    We track every expense against your budget, providing regular updates and transparent reports. We negotiate vendor pricing to ensure you get the best value without compromising quality.

    Guest List & Invitations

    We assist with guest list management, invitation design and distribution (digital or print), RSVP tracking, and seating arrangement planning.

    Timeline & Run Sheet Creation

    A minute-by-minute timeline is created for the event day, covering setup, ceremonies, entertainment, meals, and wrap-up. Every vendor and team member receives their schedule.

    Rehearsals & Walkthroughs

    We conduct rehearsals and venue walkthroughs with all key participants to ensure everyone knows their role. This eliminates confusion and ensures smooth coordination on event day.

    Final Countdown & Quality Check

    In the week leading up to your event, we conduct a comprehensive quality check — confirming all vendor bookings, reviewing décor plans, inspecting venues, and preparing backup plans.

    Event Day Execution

    Your dedicated team manages every aspect of the event — vendor arrivals, setup supervision, timeline enforcement, guest coordination, and real-time problem solving. You relax and enjoy.

    Post-Event Follow-Up

    After your celebration, we handle vendor payments, collect your photos and videos, and conduct a debrief session. Your feedback helps us continually improve and perfect our craft.

    Collaboration

    Your Role vs Our Role

    Clear expectations mean a smooth partnership from start to finish.

    👤 Your Role

    • ▶ Share your vision, preferences, and must-haves
    • ▶ Approve design concepts, vendor selections, and budget
    • ▶ Provide guest list and key participant details
    • ▶ Make timely payments as per the payment schedule
    • ▶ Show up and enjoy your celebration!

    👑 Our Role

    • ▶ Bring creative concepts and actionable plans
    • ▶ Research, vet, and coordinate all vendors
    • ▶ Manage budgets, timelines, and logistics
    • ▶ Handle all on-the-day coordination and problem solving
    • ▶ Ensure everything runs perfectly so you don't have to worry
