Our Story

Where It All Began

Aether Events was founded in 2021 with a simple belief: every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. What started as planning intimate birthday parties for friends and family quickly grew into a full-service event planning firm trusted by hundreds of clients across Tamil Nadu.

Our journey has taken us from small gatherings to grand weddings, corporate galas, and destination celebrations. Along the way, we’ve built a network of trusted vendors, developed a signature planning process, and earned the trust of families, couples, and companies who return to us year after year.

We blend timeless elegance with modern innovation, ensuring every event reflects the unique personality of those we serve. Our team of experienced planners, designers, and coordinators works closely with every client to craft celebrations that resonate long after the last guest departs.

“Every celebration is a story waiting to be told — we’re here to write yours with elegance and heart.”
