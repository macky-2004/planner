The Aether Events Story

From a simple dream to Tamil Nadu’s trusted name in celebration planning.

“Every great celebration starts with a single idea. Ours began with a birthday party and a belief that moments matter.”

Aether Events was founded in 2021 by Ananya Sharma, a passionate event professional with over a decade of experience in luxury event management. The journey began quietly — planning birthday parties for friends and family members who were amazed by the creativity and attention to detail she brought to every celebration.

Word spread quickly. Soon, Ananya was planning weddings, engagement ceremonies, and corporate events. Each event was a new opportunity to learn, grow, and refine the signature Aether approach — one that combines meticulous planning with genuine emotional connection.

By 2023, Aether Events had grown into a full-service firm with a dedicated team of planners, designers, and coordinators. We moved from a home office to a proper studio in Thanjavur and began serving clients across Tamil Nadu — from Trichy to Chennai, from Coimbatore to Madurai.

Today, we’ve planned over 500 events and built lasting relationships with hundreds of families, couples, and corporations. Our portfolio includes luxury weddings, milestone birthdays, corporate galas, intimate baby showers, and grand anniversary celebrations.

But our mission remains the same as it was on day one: to transform every celebration into a timeless memory. We believe that behind every event is a story worth telling, and we’re honoured to help our clients tell theirs.
